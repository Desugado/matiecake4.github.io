About Us

Matie Cake was born from the passion and desire to create high-quality handmade cakes, both preserving traditional flavors and bringing a modern breath. Exporting small batches of cakes from family kitchens, the brand gradually shaped its own identity: meticulousness in ingredients, sophistication in flavor and creative taste in presentation.

Through the process of research, testing and perfection, Matie Cake officially launched the market with the mission of bringing customers cakes that are not only delicious but also contain mythical spiritual values. The motto "Giving delicate gifts, sending love" has become a guiding principle in all activities, from production, product design to customer experience.

From the first challenging steps, Matie Cake has continuously developed, affirming its reputation in the handmade cake market. Nowadays, brands are not only the number 1 choice of customers when looking for a quality product but are still considered a symbol of sophistication in giving and sharing.
