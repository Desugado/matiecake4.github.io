WORKSHOP "CRAFTED BY HAND, WRAPPED WITH LOVE"

At Matie Cake, each cake is not only a delicious dish but also a bonding experience. When you buy any cake box or special gift set, you will immediately receive a voucher to join a free cake making workshop, the opportunity to make handmade cakes yourself and enjoy the joy of creativity.

Workshop Schedule

Workshop takes place on every Saturdays and Sundays.

Brand new topic every month: Moon Cake handmade, Salted egg sponge cake, flan, flan cheese, cookies, croissant and etc.

Duration: 2 - 2,5 hours per session.

Quantity: 10 – 15 people per session to ensure the best experience.

What will participants gain after joining the workshop?

Learn baking directly with Matie Cake’s chef.

Discover ingredients and secrets to making delicious, safe cakes.

Create, decorate, and bring your handmade products home.

Receive an additional café voucher to enjoy drinks after the workshop.

How to Register

Online: Register now via Matie Cake’s website or fanpage.

-Store: Present your voucher and choose a suitable participation schedule.
